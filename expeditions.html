2017

West Coast Trail

Pacific Rim National Park

May 13 - 21 2017

We felt fairly comfortable doing this trail, but had a few mishaps that could have avoided. Our tent had leaked and we did not have the materials on hand to repair it. I fell into the ocean when I made the decision to jump over a surge channel. I slipped into the water and had to be dragged out of the water by my party. Potentially would have been a very dangerous mistake, but fortunately good weather meant my risk of exposure was low and currents were not strong in this surge channel at the time. In addition, learnt the lesson of the importance of quality food on the trip. Instant pasta and mashed potatoes gets really unappetizing after more than a week on the trail. Personal hygiene on the trail is an important aspect for comfort and health in the backcountry that I did not prepare enough for. For a longer trip, I found it crucial to have a way to get somewhat clean, or it'll start to weigh on my morale.

2015

Juan de Fuca Trail

Pacific Rim National Park

August 26 - 28 2015

My very first backpacking wilderness trip ever! It was a huge learning experience, as we pushed ourselves to the very limit and making many mistakes along the way. I grew a lot from the time we spent on the Juan de Fuca trail and confirmed my desire to explore the natural world powered by my own two feet!

2013

Nootka Sound

Vancouver Island

May 2013

Very first wilderness/backcountry experience ever! Absolutely life-changing. I have no idea where I'd be today without this experience more than 5 years ago. It feels like an entirely different lifetime ago when I did this trip, as I just began to venture into the wilderness. We saw amazing wildlife and behaviours, including a deer hunt by a pack of wolves!
